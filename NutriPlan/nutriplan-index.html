The Three Metabolic Types

Protein Type

Thrives on higher protein and healthy fat intake with moderate carbohydrates. Benefits from rich, satisfying meals and quality meats.

Mixed Type

Requires balanced macronutrients with variety. Can handle moderate amounts of all food groups and benefits from dietary flexibility.

Carb Type

Functions best with higher carbohydrate intake and lower amounts of protein and fat. Thrives on fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
